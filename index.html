Rules:

The Ping Pong Game will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:

  1. The number you enter must be an integer
  2. Numbers divisible by 3 become 'ping'
  3. Numbers divisible by 5 become 'pong'
  4. Numbers divisible by both 3 and 5 become 'pingpong'
